Seaweed bread is a delightful and unique bread variation that incorporates seaweed as a key ingredient. This specialty bread showcases the flavors and nutritional benefits of edible seaweed in a delicious and innovative way.

Squid ink bread is a unique type of bread that incorporates squid ink into the dough, resulting in a distinct appearance, flavor, and aroma. Squid ink, also known as cuttlefish ink, is a dark liquid extracted from the ink sac of squid or cuttlefish.

Shrimp bread is a delectable bread variety that incorporates shrimp into the dough, infusing it with a delightful seafood flavor and adding a touch of texture. This unique bread creation combines the savory taste of shrimp with the soft and fluffy texture of bread.
